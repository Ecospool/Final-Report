1.5 Value Proposition & Novelty

1.5.1 Stakeholder Value

NUS SSU: diverts PP from incineration; reduces hazardous waste disposal cost. Student engineering teams: access to low-cost rPP filament (~$5/kg vs ~$40/kg commercial). NUS Sustainability Office: demonstrates measurable campus circular economy metric. Academic: reproducible system provides research platform for post-consumer PP rheology studies.
