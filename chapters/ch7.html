Integrated prototype overview, electrical architecture, and power budgeting.

7.1 Final Prototype Overview

The EcoSpool final prototype integrates: (1) Felfil base extruder with CR 3.5 compression screw, (2) three mica band heaters with SSR/PID control, (3) Omni-Flow annular cooling assembly, and (4) dual-axis dial-gauge monitoring station. All subsystems are mounted on a 600 × 400 mm aluminium base plate.

7.2 Electrical System

7.2.1 Heating Mechanism Electrical Architecture

Three independent SSR-PID channels control Zone 1–3 band heaters. Type-K thermocouples provide feedback. A 4-channel PID controller (230 V AC mains) interfaces with the Felfil motor controller via a shared 24 V DC bus.
