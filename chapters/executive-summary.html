Turning waste into resource

Polypropylene (PP) is one of the most widely produced plastics globally, yet recycling infrastructure for PP remains severely underdeveloped. EcoSpool addresses this gap by engineering a modified plastic extrusion system capable of transforming PP waste into reusable 3D printing filament spools. Our system integrates controlled multi-zone heating, a compression screw, and an omni-directional cooling assembly to produce consistent, high-quality filament from post-consumer PP feedstock — enabling localised, low-cost recycling at the laboratory and small-facility scale.
